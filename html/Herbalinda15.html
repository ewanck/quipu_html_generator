Herbalinda Quinde Garcia. Our town, Sondorillo is poor and very dry. So, there they encouraged us to do that …What's it called? … the sterilisation operation. Well, you have to work, There's no wage and your husband is inattentive, so one has to find a way to work. I had 7 children, and all 7 of them were small and I still had to work. When your husband ignores you and you don't have a wage, well, you have to think and work hard. And up there in the hills it is dry, There's no water, so they told me not to have too many children, because I worked hard and could fall ill. That's true.But the thing is that since the operation our health is delicate, That's the only thing, nothing more.
