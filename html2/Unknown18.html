To give my testimony about what they did...In 96 I was sterilised. I came to the Jicati Medical Centre and there they tricked me and brought me to the hospital in Huancabamba. They took a little blood from my finger, put me in a bed and then they did the operation on me, I remember it was at 7 o'clock in the evening. The next day they took me in a van, and threw me out in Nangali. From there it took me 3 days to struggle home. Then I became ill and I'm still ill today.I can't do anything. I was abandoned by my husband and my children.I am too poor to send them to school, so 3 of them left and went to the jungle. Only one of them supports me. I'm very sick, I can't do anything. The sterilisation operation has reduced me to this,I am useless. My children and my husband abandoned me because of the operation.
